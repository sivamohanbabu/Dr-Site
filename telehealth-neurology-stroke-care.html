Telehealth in Neurology & Stroke Care

Telehealth is transforming neurological healthcare by enabling patients to access expert consultations, follow-up care, and stroke guidance from the comfort of their homes.

Digital healthcare solutions are improving access to neurological care for patients across different locations. Telehealth allows patients to consult neurologists remotely for stroke management, chronic neurological conditions, second opinions, and follow-up consultations.

Dr. Ranjith G, MD (Gen Med), DM (Neuro), Post Doctoral Fellow in Stroke and Interventional Neurology, offers expert neurological guidance and patient-focused care through modern telehealth services.

Benefits of Telehealth in Neurology

  • Convenient online neurological consultations
  • Easy access to stroke follow-up care
  • Reduced travel time and hospital visits
  • Quick access to specialist opinions
  • Better continuity of care for chronic conditions

Who Can Benefit from Telehealth?

Telehealth services are beneficial for patients with stroke recovery needs, migraine, epilepsy, neurological disorders, movement disorders, and long-term neurological care requirements.

The Future of Digital Neurology Care

Telehealth is helping improve patient access, convenience, and communication in neurology by connecting patients with specialists remotely and efficiently.

Conclusion

Telehealth in neurology is making specialised healthcare more accessible and patient-friendly. It provides a safe and effective solution for ongoing neurological consultation and follow-up care.
